Personal Projects

Project-1 Bluetooth Receiver

In the picture above you can see a bluetooth surround sound system receiver, converted for use as a two channel audio amplifier - receiver. I created this after I purchased surround sound speakers that were only capable of transmitting audio via aux cable which was very annoying haha!

Project-2 3D Z3 SAT/SMT solving

Z3 is a python library which was developed at Microsoft Research it is a theorem developer which can be used for many applications
Examples: Software/hardware verification and testing, constraint solvingm, analysis of hybrid systems and security.
After joining The SEES lab at USF as a research student I began exploring the Z3 Library during June of 2022 and was fascinated capabilties, I was able to solve simple and difficult riddles and math problems
Now I have focused my research on the process of validation for VLSI system design. In essence our research revolves around the creation of autonomous methods of thread control via SAT/SMT Solvers which help us automate these methods to extract patterns from SoC execution traces whcih serve as the SoC validation and debug

Project-3 Raspberry Pi Wifi Network Configuration

When I first began mining on the helium network I made steady progress within the project, however, the project's progress came to a halt once I found out that the miners were not able to mine crypto whilst connected to an IPv6 address because Helium's blockchain could not locate the miners specific port necesarry to execute commands since multiple devices shared the same adress. As a result around 90% of the time crypto would not be mined. To solve this problem I took Raspberry pis and configured the wifi settings within the pis in order to forward all information to the newly assigned Ipv4 addresses, ports and mac addresses needed to execute Proof of witnesses
(wifi->pi->ethernet->miner)
