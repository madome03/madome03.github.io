Project-2 3D Z3 SAT/SMT solving

Z3 is a python library which was developed at Microsoft Research it is a theorem developer, which can be used for many applications
Examples: Software/hardware verification and testing, constraint solving, analysis of hybrid systems and security.
After joining The SEES lab at USF as a research student, I began exploring the Z3 Library during May of 2022, and was fascinated by it's capabilties. I was able to solve simple and difficult riddles whilst also being able to solve arithmetic and geometric math problems
Now, I have focused my research on the process of testing, verification, and validation of software, hardware, and embedded systems. My research specifically focuses on Bounded Model Checks which helps us automate the verification techniques needed in order for multi thread programs to run and cooperate efficiently via SAT/SMT Solvers. In threaded mode Z3 provides leading-edge performance, since speedup is achieved on both SAT and UNSAT instances.

Project-3 Raspberry Pi Wifi Network Configuration

When I first began mining on the helium network, I made steady progress within the project. However, the project's progress came to a halt once I found out that the miners were not able to mine crypto whilst connected to an IPv6 address. This was because Helium's blockchain could not locate the miners specific port, necesarry to execute commands since multiple devices shared the same address. As a result, around 90% of the time crypto would not be mined. To solve this problem I took Raspberry pis and configured the wifi settings, within the pis, in order to forward all information to the newly assigned Ipv4 addresses, ports and mac addresses needed to execute Proof of witnesses
(wifi->pi->ethernet->miner)
After the change was made the miners total daily crypto earned increased dramatically and equated to almost 70 United States dollars a day!!
